CORE PROGRAMS

Activities that keep Kibera inspired

Dance for Change Performances

High-energy showcases in schools, markets, and festivals that speak on gender equality, climate justice, and youth voice.

Advocacy Art

Waste Wise Saturdays

Teams cover 40 households every weekend, collecting waste, sorting plastics, and educating families on recycling.

Environment Community

Mentorship Circles

Safe spaces in schools and community hubs for boys and girls to learn life skills, menstrual health, and leadership.

Education Wellbeing

Community Library & Safe Space

In partnership with Masaku Lions Club, 35–40 children meet weekly for reading clubs, art sessions, and homework support.

Behind the scenes

How a typical Saturday unfolds

07:00 — Call time

Dancers stretch, mentors prep lesson plans, and the environment squad receives route assignments.

10:00 — Community activations

Dance pop-ups energize the neighborhood while cleanup crews collect and sort waste.

13:00 — Library sessions

Kids gather for read-alouds, art therapy, and digital literacy games.

16:00 — Debrief

Teams log impact numbers, share stories, and plan next week’s priorities.
